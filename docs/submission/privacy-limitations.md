# Privacy, safety and known limitations

## Build Week data posture

- The golden path is synthetic/de-identified and uses `example.test`
  identities, fixed UUIDs and placeholder artifact bytes.
- Original media is private. Reports expose only inspector-curated derivatives
  after a named, version/module-scoped capability check.
- Model packets contain inspector-selected evidence, not an unbounded job dump.
- OpenAI requests use `store: false`; traces and telemetry exclude evidence,
  transcripts, addresses, names, contact details, secrets and report prose.
- Validation manifests store checksums and artifact references, not the media,
  audio, transcript or report payload.
- Provider adapters default to deterministic fakes. Fake accepted/sent states
  are labelled as fake and never presented as customer delivery.

## Professional boundary

The system assists an inspector who remains responsible for observations,
classification, module completion and approval. AI suggestions are provisional,
source-linked and verified; the model has no approval or delivery path. Reports
describe observable property condition, apparent extent, uncertainty and
technical further investigation. They do not provide purchase, negotiation,
valuation, repair-cost, legal, settlement, warranty or guarantee advice.

The software does not itself prove that an inspection or report complies with
AS 4349.1, AS 4349.3, Queensland legislation or a licensing condition. The
requirement matrices are draft-unverified implementation aids. Licensed, legal,
privacy and Standards-derived review must occur before real-customer use.

## Known Build Week limitations

The following are not proven until the milestone manifest links observed
evidence:

- physical iPhone durability, termination recovery, adverse field use and
  complete onsite journey;
- live GPT-5.6 development/locked-holdout quality, latency and cost;
- a public HTTPS demo and secure named-recipient flow;
- recipient, client and assistive-technology human validation;
- logged-out video, repository and submission links.

The Build Week milestone may still complete with those observations using
synthetic data and fake/test providers. It cannot claim the following U12
outcomes:

- live professional credential verification;
- production correction/withdrawal, privileged auth or secret rotation;
- production retention/deletion/holds and measured isolated restore;
- live payment, calendar, email or report-delivery results;
- real-customer readiness, Android support-floor breadth, canonical production
  domains, licensed/legal/Standards sign-off or first revenue.

Offline capture and recovery are in scope. Offline AI, guaranteed OS background
execution and multi-device simultaneous collaboration are not promised.
