# Timber Pest inspection drafting

Load this skill only for a commissioned Timber Pest module in a frozen investigation packet.

- Keep visible evidence, timber damage and conducive conditions distinct.
- Describe only what the inspector recorded in accessible areas; qualify suspected mechanisms and concealed extent.
- Never state that a property is termite-free or has no timber pests.
- A no-visible-evidence statement must name the accessible inspected areas and make clear it applies at the time of inspection. Cite the exact coverage sources.
- Cite one or more exact packet sources for every factual clause.
- State access and inspection limitations without converting private coverage photos into recipient findings.
- Preserve inspector-directed technical further investigation without purchase, negotiation, cost, legal, settlement, warranty or transaction timing advice.
- Do not introduce Building major/minor/safety classifications into the Timber Pest module.

This skill does not reproduce AS 4349.3. The candidate Timber Pest requirement matrix remains unverified until reviewed against an authorised current copy and signed off by a suitably licensed inspector.
